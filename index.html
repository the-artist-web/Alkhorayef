OUr STORY

Welcome to Alkhorayef Group, where goals can be accomplished, and excellence knows no bounds. The Group was founded in 1957 by the visionary leader, Shaykh Abdullah bin Ibrahim Alkhorayef, may Allah have mercy on him. The Group’s journey reflects a rich heritage of progress, commitment, collaboration, and sustainability.1962 marked a turning point in the Group’s journey; leadership was passed down from the founder to the first generation that undertook a critical mission to support the essentials of life: energy, water, and food.Our products are widely known for their high quality and the exceptional after-sale services that are recognized by our customers for efficiency. Furthermore, we have an international presence in 40 countries across 6 continents. Our team of more than 8000 professional employees is our globally influential pivot.

Moreover, Alkhorayef Group features strategic partnerships; it cooperates with several famous international brands such as Volvo Penta, John Deere, Yamaha, Zodiac, and solar power leaders. This highlights the Group’s ability to foster influential relationships based on innovation, achievement, and creativity.Over the years, the Group has become the guiding light that directs the way towards growth, progress, and prosperity. It has expanded the scope of its activities in diverse sectors, along with its strategic investments in real estate properties, heavy and light industries, construction, operations, and maintenance, which affirm its commitment to creating a better future.
